MIND 3

Single-channel video, 19:40 min, 2023


Content warning: While this piece is science fiction, it contains depictions of a hate crime. Viewer discretion is advised.

In MIND 3, a memory editor at the startup MIND faces the challenging task of editing a deeply personal memory related to a hate crime for a customer. As she grapples with the emotional toll of her work, the company introduces a groundbreaking product offering new memories generated by their proprietary AI technology. Seeking an escape, she indulges in these memories, only to realize the hidden cost they carry.

MIND 3 is the final installment of the MIND still image film trilogy.
