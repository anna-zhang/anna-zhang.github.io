MIND, 1

Single-channel video, 07:22 min, 2023


Content warning: While this piece is science fiction, it contains material about a school shooting. Viewer discretion is advised.

MIND, 1 follows a single mother employed as a memory editor at MIND, a startup dedicated to making the world happier. As she assumes the responsibility of editing the memory of a school shooting survivor, both the weight of the memory itself and her own personal traumas begin to consume her, blurring the boundaries between reality and fiction.

MIND, 1 is the first part of the MIND still image film trilogy.
