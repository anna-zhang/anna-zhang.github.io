Contrast Checker

2024

CODE

An add-on for Adobe Express aimed at promoting accessible design. It allows users to easily evaluate color combinations in their designs to ensure compliance with accessibility contrast requirements. The add-on calculates the contrast ratio between foreground and background colors and assesses whether the contrast ratio meets WCAG AA and AAA accessibility standards. Try it in Adobe Express!
