Linger a While

2024

ARTCODE

As visitors spend more time on the site, stars gradually emerge. When they leave, the stars fade away, and the screen goes dark. This experience encourages visitors to slow down and linger to fully appreciate it. Each star is a hand-drawn doodle contributed by individuals from around the world, sourced from over 100,000 drawings in Google's QuickDraw dataset.
