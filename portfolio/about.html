About

Anna Zhang explores the intersection of technology and creativity. Her work takes on many forms, from creating games, fictional startups, and apps to designing photo series and AR experiences. Anna's work has been featured in Buzzfeed and Glamour, showcased at the National Museum of American History and the Fast Company Innovation Festival, and she was honored on the Forbes 30 Under 30 list.

Professionally, Anna has held roles across product management, UI/UX design, and software engineering. She seeks to envision new possibilities that emphasize creative empowerment, accessibility, and community.

Anna studied Computer Science and Art at Yale University.
