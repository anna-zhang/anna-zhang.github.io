Anna Zhang

(she/her)

b. 2001, New York, USA

Anna Zhang is an artist whose work focuses on imaging and reimagining our relationships with technology. Her interests lay in new media, speculative fiction, games, creative tools, and digital accessibility.

Her projects include creating a fictional startup that explores the invisible labor underpinning technology, designing a mobile game that encourages positive actions and rewards kindness, and improving accessibility for America's open-source code platform.

Anna's work has been featured in various locations online (Royal Photographic Journal, Real Life, New Media Caucus, Buzzfeed, Forbes) and offline (National Museum of American History, CICA). She is a recipient of the Judson-Morrissey Excellence in New Media Award and was honored on the Forbes 30 Under 30 List. She also takes photographs and has been published in magazines like Glamour and Porter.

She holds a Bachelor of Arts in Computing and the Arts from Yale University.
