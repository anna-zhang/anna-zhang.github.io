Brightlove

2019

DESIGNCREATIVE DIRECTION

Brightlove is a 2D platforming runner that encourages players to be kind and to take action, rather than be mere bystanders. A refreshing contrast to the violence that often permeates mobile games, Brightlove encourages positive actions and rewards kindness. Featured on Forbes, Google Play, and Buzzfeed. Download Brightlove on Google Play!
